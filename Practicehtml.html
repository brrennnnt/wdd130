The man who got 3 free beers

A guy walks into a bar and asks for a drink

The bartender gives him his beer and the man tells the bartender,

"I will show you the coolest thing you have ever seen if you give me a free beer."

The bartender replies,

"I'm intrigued, why not?"

The man pulls out a little tiny real life man out of his pocket

The bartender was amazed and gave him a free drink

The man said he'd show him something even better for another free drink

The bartender agreed and the man pulled out a tiny piano for the tiny man to play

Amazed again, the bartender gave the man a second free drink

The man told the bartender a magic Genie created them and for a third free drink the bartender could have a wish

The bartender agreed and wished for a million bucks

Suddenly duck started falling from the sky filling up the bar and surrounding streets

Confused the bartender exclaimed,

"I said a million Bucks not Ducks!"

To which the man replied,

"Well you didn't think I wished for a ten inch pianist did you?"
