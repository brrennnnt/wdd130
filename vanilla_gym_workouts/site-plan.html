Content

Home page

The home page will consist of an overall schedule. It will show Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday. Next to Monday it will say Bench and Squat. Tuesday will say Shoulders and forearms OR Biceps and Triceps. Wednesday will say Deadlift and Powerclean. Thursday will say Legs and Abs. Friday will say Decline Bench & Front Squat. The home page will also specify if the overall workout for that day is a CORE or an AUXILIARY. If it is a CORE the user will go to page 2 and learn how to do that workout. If the workout is an AUXILIARY, the user will go to page 3 to learn about auxiliary workouts.

Images for the Home page (they will be smaller)

Core Workouts

This page will consist of how to do the workouts for monday, wednesday, and friday, because they are all core workouts. It will teach how to bench, how to squat, how to deadlift, how to powerclean, how to decline bench, and how to front squat.

Images for Core Workouts

Auxiliary Workouts

This page will teach the user how to do all of the auxiliary workouts. These workouts are done on tuesday and thursday. This will go into detail on how to workout your shoulders and chest, biceps and triceps, and legs & abs. These 3 workouts consist of 5-8 specific workouts each to do with dumbbells or other gym equipment. This page will explain each one and demonstrate how to do the workout.
